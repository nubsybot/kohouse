I started recording song tapes before becoming a teenager and a weekly highlight was watching one of the first music video shows Monday night with my sister Betty, called "Formula One"!
Years later at a Saturday club night in the early 90's, one of my best friends I went to school with invited a bunch of mates to an after hour session to his place to watch the first F1 race of the season hosted in Melbourne !
There was a dj mixer and two 1200 Technics turntables set up and there were some really cool 12" vinyl, too. For the first time in my life I tried to mix music, simultaneously playing two records .
I never stopped 'djing' after that day: "follow your passion is my motto !"
Underneath you can see three cover pictures, created by my friend Simon Martin (film director/artist) of my DJ sets that you can choose from, enjoy listening, bye bye DJ LLdiSL.
